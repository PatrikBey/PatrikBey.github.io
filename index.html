BN

BRAIN NETWORKS

In my research I focus on mathematical representations of high dimensional and highly complex neuroimaging data using various network models. This allows us to study properties of the brain on different levels of abstraction and over different modalities such as structural and functional imaging.

  • Connectomics
  • Graph Theory
  • Multi-Modal Data Integration
LD.

LESION DYNAMICS

Brain lesions can have a major impact on brain functionality and vary strongly in their spatial and temporal properties. To this end we study the temporal dynamics of lesioned tissue and its varying impact on brain function.

  • Brain Lesion
  • Temporal Dynamics
  • Data Processing
TR.

TRANSLATIONAL RESEARCH

Translational research aims at enabling the application of basic research from animals to the patients hospital bed. Finding comparable animal models to human brain diseases represents a major bottleneck. USing brain networks and simulation we try to establish a framework for cross species model comparison.

  • Cross-Species Comparability
  • Translation Biomarker
  • Mouse Brain Simulation
